How Can I Help?

  • Donate blood: During this process I am unable to make enough blood and platelets to support myself, and I rely on transfusions from blood donors to keep me alive. I owe my life to anonymous blood donors, and if you donate blood and encourage others to do the same, it goes a long way toward helping people like me!
  • Join the bone marrow registry: Bone marrow is what makes blood, and my bone marrow is making bad blood. If someone with the right genetic match donates some of their bone marrow to me, I can make their blood in my body, and my cancer could be cured! You can potentially save the life of someone like me by signing up to become a bone marrow donor at nmdp.org. Signing up is free, and it just requires a cheeck swab test you send in through the mail.
